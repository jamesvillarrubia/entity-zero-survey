Your task: pairwise tone comparisons

For each conversation, you'll see 6 paired comparisons. Each compares two dimensions of Luca's tone — e.g., "Was the conversation more warm or more engaged?" — on a 4-point scale:

Strongly A  |  Somewhat A  |  Somewhat B  |  Strongly B

You're forced to choose between A and B on every question even when they feel similar — pick whichever seems closer.

The four dimensions

  • Energy — vigor / battery. Depleted (1) ↔ animated (5).
  • Engagement — participation: elaborates, asks back. Withdrawn ↔ curious.
  • Warmth — emotional availability. Distant / cool ↔ warm / affectionate.
  • Rumination — stuck-in-her-head. Present-focused ↔ preoccupied.

These dimensions are independent. Examples: "i'm exhausted but i love you, text me later ok ❤️" = low energy + high warmth. "OMG SO BUSY can't talk bye" = high energy + low warmth.

Don't reward or penalize length. Rate the overall character of Luca's voice across the 4 turns.
